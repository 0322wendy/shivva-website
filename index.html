Shivva is a shoegaze band from Miami, FL who focus on dreamy and ethereal sounds contrasting with distorted and droning lashes of noise. They are inspired by old and new shoegaze/alternative bands such as My Bloody Valentine, Slowdive, Jesus and Mary Chain, Ringo Deathstarr, and Slow Crush.
